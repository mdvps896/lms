# Privacy Policy for MD Consultancy

**Effective Date: January 21, 2026**

MD Consultancy ("we," "us," or "our") operates the MD Consultancy mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:
*   Email address
*   First name and last name
*   Phone number (Mobile)
*   Address, State, Province, ZIP/Postal code, City

#### Images and Camera Access
Our app includes identity verification features. We may collect:
*   **Selfie Images:** To verify your identity during course enrollment and exam attempts.
*   **Camera Permission:** To capture these verification images in real-time.

#### Usage Data
We may also collect information on how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This includes information such as your device's IP address, device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

## 2. Use of Data

MD Consultancy uses the collected data for various purposes:
*   To provide and maintain our Service (Course access, Exams).
*   To verify user identity through selfie captures during sensitive activities.
*   To notify you about changes to our Service.
*   To provide customer support.
*   To monitor the usage of our Service and detect security threats like unauthorized multiple-device logins.
*   To process payments through our third-party partners.

## 3. Transfer of Data

Your information, including Personal Data, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, or country where the data protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction.

## 4. Disclosure of Data

We may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:
*   To comply with a legal obligation.
*   To protect and defend the rights or property of MD Consultancy.
*   To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service.
*   To protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.

## 5. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us. We use secure authentication methods (JWT, OTP) and industry-standard encryption, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 6. Service Providers (Third-Party)

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service:
*   **Firebase (Google):** For push notifications and mobile OTP verification.
*   **Razorpay:** For secure payment processing.
*   **Google Auth:** For streamlined user registration and login.

## 7. App Permissions

To provide full functionality, the app requests:
*   **Camera:** For identity verification.
*   **Storage:** To download and save study materials (PDFs).
*   **Internet:** To access our online services.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
